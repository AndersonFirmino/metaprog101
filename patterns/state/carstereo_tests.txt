======================================
CarStero: State design pattern example
======================================

  >>> from carstereo_classic import Receiver
  >>> rcv = Receiver()
  >>> rcv
  <Receiver source: FM 89.1 (play)>
  >>> rcv.skip()
  >>> rcv
  <Receiver source: FM 90.5 (play)>
  >>> rcv.pause()
  >>> rcv
  <Receiver source: FM 90.5 (mute)>
  >>> rcv.change_source()
  >>> rcv
  <Receiver source: AM 620 (play)>
  >>> rcv.change_source()
  >>> rcv
  <Receiver source: CD track #1:"And I Love Her" (play)>
  >>> rcv.skip()
  >>> rcv
  <Receiver source: CD track #2:"Any Time at All" (play)>
  >>> rcv.pause()
  >>> rcv
  <Receiver source: CD track #2:"Any Time at All" (pause)>
  >>> rcv.play()
  >>> rcv
  <Receiver source: CD track #2:"Any Time at All" (play)>
